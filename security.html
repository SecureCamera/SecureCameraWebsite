Security First Philosophy

SnapSafe was built from the ground up with security as our primary concern. Unlike many photo apps that add security as an afterthought, every feature in SnapSafe is designed with your privacy in mind.

Our approach considers multiple threat models, from casual snooping to sophisticated forensic tools. We believe your photos belong to you and only you should control who sees them and when.
